Guns

  • Vandal: A powerful rifle that delivers high damage with each shot, effective at all ranges.
  • Phantom: A silenced rifle that excels in close to medium range, with a high fire rate.
  • Operator: A high-powered sniper rifle that can kill an enemy with a single shot to the body.
  • Guardian: A semi-automatic rifle that provides accuracy and damage at long distances.
  • Bucky: A shotgun that is deadly at close range, capable of taking down enemies quickly.
  • Judge: A fully automatic shotgun that can spray bullets in close quarters for high damage.
  • Stinger: A submachine gun that offers a high rate of fire, ideal for aggressive plays.
  • Spectre: A silenced submachine gun that is effective in close to medium range engagements, offering a good balance of fire rate and accuracy.
  • Marshal: A lever-action sniper rifle that deals high damage and is effective for players who prefer precision over rapid fire.
  • Ares: A light machine gun with a high magazine capacity and a slower fire rate, effective for suppressing enemies.
  • Odin: A heavy machine gun that boasts a massive magazine and high damage output, perfect for area denial and suppressive fire.
  • Classic: The default pistol available at the start of each round, offering decent damage and accuracy.
  • Shorty: A compact shotgun that is great for close-quarters combat, allowing for quick kills at close range.
  • Frenzy: A semi-automatic pistol with a high fire rate, effective for aggressive plays and surprise attacks.
  • Ghost: A silenced pistol that is effective for stealthy plays, capable of delivering high damage with headshots.
  • Knife: The default melee weapon, which can be used for quick kills when players are in close proximity to enemies.
